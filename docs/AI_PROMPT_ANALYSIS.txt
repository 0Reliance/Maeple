# AI Prompt Accuracy Analysis & Research

Date: December 26, 2025
Purpose: Critical review of AI prompts for psychological safety

## Critical Finding

The current geminiService.ts prompts ask AI to "detect" inherently subjective states like "masking" and "sensory load" which violates our objective design principles.

## Required Action: Rewrite all AI prompts to focus on EXTRACTING objective data, not DETECTING subjective states.
