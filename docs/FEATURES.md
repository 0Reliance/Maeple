### Multi-Dimensional Capacity Grid
Forget the linear "1-10" energy scale. We use a **7-point Capacity Grid** to track your energy budget across different domains:
-   **Focus**: Cognitive bandwidth.
-   **Social**: Capacity for interaction.
-   **Sensory**: Tolerance for noise, light, and texture.
-   **Emotional**: Resilience and regulation.
-   **Physical**: Body energy and pain levels.
-   **Structure**: Need for routine vs. flexibility.
-   **Executive**: Ability to plan and initiate tasks.

### Capacity Metrics (The Lexicon)
We use precise language to describe your state:
-   **Bandwidth**: Your available capacity in a domain.
-   **Load**: The current demand placed on you.
-   **Interference**: When one stressed system disrupts another (e.g., Sensory Overload ‚Üí Emotional Crash).
-   **Noise Generators**: Hidden costs like Masking or Perfectionism.

### The "Spoon" Theory Integration
Native support for "Spoon Theory" tracking, allowing you to visualize your daily energy expenditure and recovery.
