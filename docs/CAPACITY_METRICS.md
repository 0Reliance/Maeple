---

## 1. The 7 Domains of Capacity

We track capacity across seven distinct domains, recognizing that neurodivergent individuals often have uneven profiles (spiky profiles).

1.  **Focus**: Cognitive bandwidth for attention and deep work.
2.  **Social**: Capacity for interaction, communication, and presence.
3.  **Sensory**: Tolerance for noise, light, texture, and environmental stimuli.
4.  **Emotional**: Resilience, regulation, and ability to process feelings.
5.  **Physical**: Body energy, pain levels, and stamina.
6.  **Structure**: Need for routine, predictability, and external order.
7.  **Executive**: Ability to plan, initiate, switch tasks, and regulate impulses.

## 3. The Goal: Pattern Literacy

The ultimate goal of MAEPLE is not to "fix" these numbers but to help users recognize their unique patterns.

> **Example Insight**:
> "Your **Executive Bandwidth** is low today, but **Masking Noise** is high. This creates **Interference** that puts you on a steep **Burnout Trajectory**. Recommendation: Reduce **Sensory Load** to preserve remaining bandwidth."
